Lentejas con chorizo

Description

Recipe based around lentils and chorizo ,a typical spanish sausage, stewed together with potatoes and carrots among other vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Chorizo
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Bell pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Sweet paprika

Steps

  1. Saut√© a chopped onion and a chopped bell pepper in abundant olive oil.
  2. Add the lentils previously soaked for about an hour and drained
  3. Add diced potatoes and carrots
  4. Add the chorizo thickly sliced and broth or water to cover
  5. Let it simmer on medium/low heat for about an hour or until everything is cooked
