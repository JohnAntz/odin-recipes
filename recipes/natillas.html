Natillas

Description

Dessert known in english as custard. Thickened and sweetened egg and milk mixture.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Corn starch

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs, sugar, corn starch and vanilla extract together.
  2. Heat the milk but don't let it simmer
  3. Add a little of the milk to the eggs and wisk it together to warm the eggs
  4. Add the eggs to the milk and wisk until thickened
  5. Let it cool and put some ground cinnamon on top.
