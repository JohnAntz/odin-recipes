Arroz con leche

Description

Dessert made with rice and milk.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Lemon peel

Steps

  1. Put the milk, cinnamon stick and lemon peel on the stove to infuse
  2. Add the rice once the milk has infused and stir every 2/3 minutes so it doesnt stick to the pot
  3. Add the sugar and keep mixing it until its fully dissolved
  4. Let it cool and put some ground cinnamon on top.
